 **🍽️ FoodOps AI**

**FoodOps AI** is an AI-powered food operations platform designed to help food manufacturers improve food safety, quality management, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency through intelligent automation.

Instead of solving a single problem, FoodOps AI combines multiple AI modules into one integrated platform that supports quality assurance and food safety teams.

---

## Vision

To build an intelligent assistant for the food industry capable of analyzing complaints, monitoring recalls, generating CAPA reports, assisting audits, evaluating supplier risks, and supporting food safety decision-making.

---

## Platform Modules

### ✅ Complaint Intelligence

AI-powered customer complaint analysis.

**Features**

* Complaint classification
* Severity prediction
* Root cause analysis
* Corrective action recommendations
* Preventive action recommendations
* High-risk email alerts

**Status:** Completed

---

### ✅ Recall Intelligence

AI-powered FDA food recall monitoring.

**Features**

* FDA RSS monitoring
* AI recall analysis
* Hazard identification
* Risk classification
* Recall database
* High-risk email notifications

**Status:** Completed

---

### 🚧 CAPA Assistant

AI-generated Corrective and Preventive Action reports.

Planned Features:

* Root cause generation
* Corrective actions
* Preventive actions
* Responsible department
* Priority assessment
* Verification methods
* Due date recommendations
* CAPA report generation

**Status:** In Development

---

### 🤖 Food Safety AI Copilot
